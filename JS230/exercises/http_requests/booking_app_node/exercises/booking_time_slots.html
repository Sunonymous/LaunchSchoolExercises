Exercise: Booking Time Slots

Goal: Provide a way for students to book time slots, and for new students to be created.

Details:

  • Students may click the dropdown menu to load available schedules.
  • Students enter their email address to connect the schedule with themselves.
  • If the email provided is unregistered to any student, another menu appears to create a new student.
  • Use the booking sequence provided by the server response to create the student.
  • Two alerts appear upon success: one if a student is created, and another if a schedule is booked.
